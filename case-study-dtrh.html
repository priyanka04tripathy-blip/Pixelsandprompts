→ The Situation

A magical wonderland space.
Genuinely thoughtful.
Entirely invisible online.

When the team at Down The Rabbit Hole reached out, they had built something truly special: a beautifully designed, Alice in Wonderland-themed indoor play space in the heart of Bangalore. From a parent-friendly workspace to creative sensory play arenas, it was thoughtfully designed, magical, and highly safe.

The bottleneck: They had zero digital presence. 1 post. 8 followers. No strategy.

A space this good deserved an audience that knew it existed. Families in Bangalore were actively looking for creative spaces, but they had no way of finding DTRH. That is the hurdle we jumped over together.

→ The Brief

Create a destination,
not just a kids play area.

The founders made it clear: they didn’t want a generic corporate "kids zone" Instagram feed. They wanted an online identity that mirrors the actual warmth of the space.

The primary goal was to capture the ambient visual magic, build instant trust with protective parents, and convert scrolling attention into active physical bookings for birthday slots, summer camp admissions, and play sessions.

→ Our Approach

Dual Action Strategy: Priyanka & Rashi Collaboration

An equal parts blend of strict audience analytics and creative world-building.

Strategy & Performance

Priyanka took the wheel on:

  • ✦ Audience Deep-Dive: Analysing search trends and local parenting demographics in Bangalore.
  • ✦ Content Framework: Structuring intentional organic content pillars and monthly editorial calendars.
  • ✦ Copywriting: Crafting high-conversion, warm, and highly informative caption copy.
  • ✦ Paid Campaigns: Constructing, optimizing, and deploying laser-targeted ad conversion funnels.
Creative Direction

Rashi took the wheel on:

  • ✦ Visual Identity: Developing an elegant, fairy-tale themed creative suite based on Wonderland.
  • ✦ Aesthetic Guidelines: Establishing color-graded photo setups and consistent fonts.
  • ✦ Graphic Templates: Designing reusable posts, animated carousels, and cohesive highlights.
  • ✦ Ad Creatives: Generating high-contrast graphic flyers and assets for summer camps and birthday parties.

→ Lessons Learned

What This Taught Us

Down The Rabbit Hole was one of the most exciting projects we’ve worked on — not just because the results were strong, but because we got to build everything from scratch.

Starting from zero is actually a gift. There are no old habits to undo, no mismatched visuals to clean up, and no conflicting messages to untangle. You get to define the story from the very first post.

What worked beautifully here was the combination of a clear brand direction, consistent content, and ads that were built on top of an organic foundation rather than instead of one. The organic content gave us proof of what resonated. The ads amplified what was already working.

That's the system. And it works.
