→ The Situation

A doctor with 30 years of expertise.
An Instagram that was not keeping up.

Dr. Sheela Nambiar is the kind of expert you want in your corner. A qualified OB/GYN, lifestyle medicine physician, positive psychology practitioner, and fitness consultant — she has spent decades helping women build healthier, more fulfilling lives.

Her expertise? Undeniable.
Her Instagram? Not quite keeping up.

When she came to us, the content was not reflecting the depth of what she offers. There was no consistent visual identity, no clear content direction, and the page was not building the kind of trust that her work deserved.

Women with her level of authority should have a digital presence that matches it.

That is where we came in.

→ The Ask

What we were building toward.

01

Create a clear brand identity foundation for a medical and lifestyle expert

02

Build a consistent Instagram presence that feels credible and human

03

Improve reach and visibility through a repeatable content engine

04

Establish content pillars that make her expertise easy to understand and follow

→ How We Worked

Two people. Two disciplines.
One unified brand.

Before any content went live, we started with the audience. Dr. Sheela's followers are women at various stages of life — navigating questions about fitness, hormonal health, nutrition, mental wellbeing, and aging. They are not looking for clinical jargon. They want reliable, warm, actionable guidance from someone who actually knows what she is talking about.

So we built content that lived at the intersection of those two things: deeply informed, but genuinely readable.

→ How We Did It

Month by month.
No surprises.

1
Month 1

Foundation

We started with the audience and the brand. Rashi built the full visual identity system — palette, typography, and design direction. Priyanka mapped the content pillars and built the editorial calendar. Before anything went live, the strategy was solid.

2
Month 2

Build

The feed went live. Templates were built and handed over to a repeatable system. Every post was crafted to educate without overwhelming — useful enough to save, clear enough to understand on first read.

3
Month 3

Grow and Refine

With consistent output, the data started talking. We refined what was resonating, doubled down on the content types driving reach, and kept the posting rhythm tight. No weeks off. No dropped balls.

4
Month 4+

Sustained Growth

Organic reach crossed 3,500 accounts per month. Impressions hit 15,460. Reels grew from 417 to 1,174. Stories from 287 to 678. The system was working — and it kept working.

→ The Takeaway

Brand identity first.
Content second.
Always.

Dr. Sheela's account reminded us why brand identity work matters before content work. The moment we had a clear visual system and content direction, everything else became faster, more consistent, and more effective.

It also taught us something we now tell every client: when your content looks like you and sounds like you, your audience knows exactly who they are following. That trust compounds. Every post, every story, every reel adds to it.

"That is how 1,018 accounts becomes 3,555. And how 15,000 impressions becomes the new floor, not the ceiling."
